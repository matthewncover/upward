Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 10, 2025

Overview

Upward Habits is a personal habit tracking application designed for individual use. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • WHOOP Data: Heart rate variability (HRV), recovery scores, sleep scores, and daily strain data accessed through the WHOOP API
  • Habit Data: Personal habit tracking information you manually enter, including reading progress, breathwork duration, and screen time usage
  • Usage Data: Basic application usage information to maintain service functionality

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Calculating your personal habit tracking scores and metrics
  • Generating visualizations of your progress over time
  • Sending optional email notifications about habit tracking
  • Maintaining and improving the application's functionality

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our server infrastructure. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used exclusively for your personal habit tracking and is not accessible to other users or external parties.

Data Retention

We retain your habit tracking data indefinitely to provide you with long-term progress tracking and historical analysis. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

WHOOP Integration

When you connect your WHOOP account, we access only the specific data types listed above through WHOOP's official API. We do not access any other WHOOP data beyond what is necessary for habit tracking calculations.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we have about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
